## Access Control & Permissions

**Permission tiers:**

- **`full`** — all potential investors after signed NDA
- **`restricted`** — only qualified investors (LP / GP) after Engagement Letter
- **`restricted (high)`** — only Big-4 vendor-DD team + tier-1 legal-opinion firm + Series A lead
- **`Q&A-only`** — the document is not handed over; answers to questions are provided through the formal Q&A channel
- **`no-access`** — internal only

## Disclosure Letter Template

The Disclosure Letter is delivered together with the SPA / SHA project and explicitly discloses all material exceptions from the standard warranties. The template is located in `01_Corporate/Disclosure_Letter_template.md` (to be prepared at T+1 month together with the SHA).

## VDR Tool Selection (for production)

For the current round we recommend one of:
- **Intralinks** (industry standard, used by the Big Four)
- **Datasite** (formerly Merrill Datasite)
- **Firmex** (mid-market, good UX)
- **iDeals** (cost-effective)

Self-hosted alternatives for post Series A rounds: **Cylynx VDR**, **Onehub**, **SecureDocs**.

## Q&A Process

- Q&A only via the VDR Q&A tool (no email channel).
- All questions are logged.
- Answers within ≤ 48 h of receiving the question.
- Q&A is managed by the Compliance Officer + General Counsel + relevant subject-matter expert.
- A daily Q&A digest is sent to all active interest parties.

## Audit Trail

The VDR tool automatically logs:
- Who opened the document
- How long it was viewed
- Whether it was downloaded (for tier `restricted (high)` — downloading is disabled)
- Screen captures (with watermarking)

Audit Trail review takes place quarterly within the Audit Committee.

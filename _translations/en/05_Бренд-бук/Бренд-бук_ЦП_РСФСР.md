## 2. Tone of Voice (rules of speech)

**Do:**
1. Speak with facts and regulatory references.
2. Use full forms — "the Russian Federation", "the Central Bank of the Russian Federation", "the Federal Treasury".
3. Maintain an institutional tempo — sentence length is medium, free of clichés.
4. Apply neutral verbs: ensures, executes, regulates, coordinates.
5. Every numeric indicator is provided with a reference to the source.

**Do not:**
1. Do not use marketing superlatives ("the most advanced", "revolutionary").
2. Do not use emojis in the main text of documents.
3. Do not write the words "version / v1 / draft / final".
4. Do not employ nostalgic or politicised connotations.
5. Do not personalise communications with the CBR, the Ministry of Finance or the Government — only the official register.

## 3. Logo

### 3.1. Concept
A monogram "R" (from RSFSR), set into a strict geometric mark — a combination of a **shield-seal** (state-level gravity) and a **digital point / pixel** (technological nature). The mark operates on three semantic levels: sovereignty → institution → digital.

The corporate pack contains two concepts (for approval):
- **Concept A** — a square seal with a corner digital point.
- **Concept B** — a circular seal with a dashed contour and a digital register row.

All variants are placed in a single SVG file, `Логотип-концепция.svg`.

### 3.2. Clear space and minimum size
- Clear space: not less than ½ of the height of the main mark on all sides.
- Minimum size: 32 px in height on digital carriers; 12 mm in print.

### 3.3. Versions
- Primary (colour): on a light background.
- Mono: 100% black on white.
- Inverse: white on a dark background (Primary Dark).
- Favicon 32×32, 16×16.

### 3.4. Forbidden uses
- Distortion of proportions.
- Rotation by more than 0°.
- Application of shadows, outlines, gradients outside the tokens.
- Placement on a busy background without a backplate.

## 6. Grid and proportions

- Web: 12-column grid, container 1200 px, gap 24 px.
- Tablet: 8-column, container 100% – 64 px, gap 16 px.
- Mobile: 4-column, container 100% – 32 px, gap 12 px.
- Baseline grid: 4 px.
- Spacing scale: 0 / 4 / 8 / 12 / 16 / 24 / 32 / 48 / 64 / 96 / 128.

### Radii
- xs 4 px, sm 8 px, md 12 px, lg 16 px, xl 24 px, pill 9999 px.

### Shadows
- elev-1: `0 1px 2px rgba(11, 43, 92, 0.06), 0 1px 1px rgba(11, 43, 92, 0.04)`.
- elev-2: `0 4px 8px rgba(11, 43, 92, 0.08), 0 2px 4px rgba(11, 43, 92, 0.04)`.
- elev-3: `0 12px 24px rgba(11, 43, 92, 0.10), 0 4px 8px rgba(11, 43, 92, 0.06)`.

### Motion
- 120 ms — micro (hover, focus).
- 200 ms — standard (menu opening, transitions).
- 320 ms — emphasis (modal windows, page transitions).
- Easing: `cubic-bezier(0.2, 0, 0, 1)`.

Brand Book

Digital Platform “Russian Service for Social Development Financing” (CPFSR)

Know-how rightsholder: Abdul-Khakim Akhmadovich Kagirov. Deposit certificate No. 4011265 of 19.12.2024 (ireg.pro), hash GOST R 34.11-2012. Purpose of the document: a unified visual identification system for the platform. Applied across all carriers: documents, presentations, the website, the mobile application, advertising materials, corporate merchandise.

1. Brand platform

Attribute Value
Mission To provide off-budget targeted financing of national and infrastructure projects through digital financial assets backed by collateral, within the current legal framework of the Russian Federation (Stage 1, pilot-light 2026–2028) with the horizon of mandate expansion (Stage 2, 2029+).
Vision To become the sovereign front-end / agent platform for targeted infrastructure DFA: in Stage 1 — through technology partnership with 779-P operators and infrastructure funding from the VEB.RF Factory + the Federal Treasury; in Stage 2 — a defensible path to the model of targeted secured emission by the Bank of Russia.
Brand essence A state-level technology operator of trust.
Archetype Sovereign / Statesman (primary). Secondary — Caregiver (social responsibility).
Emotional register Calm assurance, institutionality, technological maturity. No pomp, no Soviet nostalgia, no populism.

2. Tone of Voice (rules of speech)

Do: 1. Speak with facts and regulatory references. 2. Use full forms — “the Russian Federation”, “the Central Bank of the Russian Federation”, “the Federal Treasury”. 3. Maintain an institutional tempo — sentence length is medium, free of clichés. 4. Apply neutral verbs: ensures, executes, regulates, coordinates. 5. Every numeric indicator is provided with a reference to the source.

Do not: 1. Do not use marketing superlatives (“the most advanced”, “revolutionary”). 2. Do not use emojis in the main text of documents. 3. Do not write the words “version / v1 / draft / final”. 4. Do not employ nostalgic or politicised connotations. 5. Do not personalise communications with the CBR, the Ministry of Finance or the Government — only the official register.

3. Logo

3.1. Concept

A monogram “R” (from RSFSR), set into a strict geometric mark — a combination of a shield-seal (state-level gravity) and a digital point / pixel (technological nature). The mark operates on three semantic levels: sovereignty → institution → digital.

The corporate pack contains two concepts (for approval): - Concept A — a square seal with a corner digital point. - Concept B — a circular seal with a dashed contour and a digital register row.

All variants are placed in a single SVG file, Логотип-концепция.svg.

3.2. Clear space and minimum size

  • Clear space: not less than ½ of the height of the main mark on all sides.
  • Minimum size: 32 px in height on digital carriers; 12 mm in print.

3.3. Versions

  • Primary (colour): on a light background.
  • Mono: 100% black on white.
  • Inverse: white on a dark background (Primary Dark).
  • Favicon 32×32, 16×16.

3.4. Forbidden uses

  • Distortion of proportions.
  • Rotation by more than 0°.
  • Application of shadows, outlines, gradients outside the tokens.
  • Placement on a busy background without a backplate.

5.3. Behaviour

  • Numbers in tables — aligned to digit groups (font-variant-numeric: tabular-nums).
  • Headings — without inter-letter expansion.
  • Body — slight expansion -0.005em for better readability.

6. Grid and proportions

  • Web: 12-column grid, container 1200 px, gap 24 px.
  • Tablet: 8-column, container 100% – 64 px, gap 16 px.
  • Mobile: 4-column, container 100% – 32 px, gap 12 px.
  • Baseline grid: 4 px.
  • Spacing scale: 0 / 4 / 8 / 12 / 16 / 24 / 32 / 48 / 64 / 96 / 128.

Radii

  • xs 4 px, sm 8 px, md 12 px, lg 16 px, xl 24 px, pill 9999 px.

Shadows

  • elev-1: 0 1px 2px rgba(11, 43, 92, 0.06), 0 1px 1px rgba(11, 43, 92, 0.04).
  • elev-2: 0 4px 8px rgba(11, 43, 92, 0.08), 0 2px 4px rgba(11, 43, 92, 0.04).
  • elev-3: 0 12px 24px rgba(11, 43, 92, 0.10), 0 4px 8px rgba(11, 43, 92, 0.06).

Motion

  • 120 ms — micro (hover, focus).
  • 200 ms — standard (menu opening, transitions).
  • 320 ms — emphasis (modal windows, page transitions).
  • Easing: cubic-bezier(0.2, 0, 0, 1).

7. Iconography

  • Style: linear, stroke 1.5 px, base size 24×24 px (viewBox 24).
  • Corners: radius 1 px.
  • Default colour: Primary #0B2B5C or Neutral 700.
  • Active state: Accent fill.

Base set (12 icons): document, house, coin, chain (DLT), escrow vault, CBR seal, mobile, charts, lock-shield, key-biometrics, handshake, settlement-scales.

8. Illustrations

  • Geometric abstractions: grids, isometry, minimal Primary → Primary Light gradients.
  • No stock photographs of people.
  • Permitted: photographs of infrastructure objects in documentary style (a bridge, power lines, the Bank of Russia building) — post-processed in cool tones.

9. Document style

Word / PDF

  • Margins: top 25 mm, bottom 25 mm, left 30 mm, right 15 mm.
  • Header: Primary stripe 4 mm + document title (Inter 9 pt, Primary).
  • Footer: Inter 8 pt Neutral 500: “Digital Platform CPFSR · A.-Kh. A. Kagirov · Deposit certificate No. 4011265” — on the left; page number — on the right.
  • Headings: Onest, Primary.
  • Tables: alternating Neutral 100 / White, header Primary 14 pt in white.
  • Quotations: vertical Accent 2 px bar on the left, Neutral 700 text.

Presentations

Slide templates: - Title: dark Primary Dark background, white heading, corner gold mark. - Table of contents: 12 items with thin separators, numbering in Accent digits. - Content: 1 H2 + 2 columns. - Quote: a large phrase in white on Primary, signature in Accent. - Numeric: large Accent figures, description in Body M. - Roadmap: a horizontal band with stages. - Contacts: dark background, details in one column.

13. Legal protection

All elements of the corporate identity (the logo, the monogram, the colour concept, typography tokens) are part of the author’s protected know-how under Deposit certificate No. 4011265 of 19.12.2024. Use without the rightsholder’s written consent is prohibited.

14. Compliance control

Every material issued under the CPFSR brand passes a three-tier check-list:

  1. Structural — alignment with the template and the grid.
  2. Colour and typography — palette and typography exclusively from the design tokens.
  3. Content — Tone of Voice, no forbidden phrases, accuracy of regulatory references.

The Brand Book is prepared as part of the Digital Platform CPFSR documentation pack and is an internal document governing the visual and verbal identity of the project.

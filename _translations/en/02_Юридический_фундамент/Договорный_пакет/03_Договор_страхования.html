Insurance Contract

Автор: Кагиров А.-Х.А. · Свидетельство о депонировании №4011265 от 19.12.2024 · Center Group Company · Engagement: CPFSR-RF-DESIGN-2026

Insurance Contract for the DFA and the Subject of Pledge (framework draft)

Subject: insurance against the risk of loss, damage and destruction of the Subject of Pledge and against the platform operator’s liability risk. Legal basis: Law of the RF No. 4015-1 of 27.11.1992 “On the Organisation of Insurance Business in the Russian Federation”; Chapter 48 “Insurance” of the Civil Code of the RF; Federal Law No. 40-FZ of 25.04.2002 (applicable for vehicles).

1. Parties

1.1. The Insured — JSC “CP RSFSR” (the Operator) or the Pledgor (depending on the scheme).

1.2. The Insurer — an insurance organisation holding a Bank of Russia licence to carry out property insurance and/or financial-risks insurance (by class of insurance — on the basis of a CBR Directive).

1.3. The Beneficiary — the DFA holder (via the Operator’s settlement mechanism) upon the occurrence of an insured event in respect of the Subject of Pledge.

2. Object of insurance

2.1. Subject of Pledge — property interests connected with the possession, use and disposal of the Subject of Pledge.

2.2. Operator’s liability — the Operator’s property interests connected with the obligation to compensate harm caused (professional-liability insurance of a financial-infrastructure operator).

3. Insured events

3.1. For the Subject of Pledge — real estate: - fire, lightning strike, gas explosion; - natural disasters (flood, earthquake, hurricane); - wrongful acts of third parties (theft, arson, robbery); - water flooding from adjoining premises; - the fall of aircraft / their parts.

3.2. For the Subject of Pledge — a vehicle: CASCO-standard (theft + damage).

3.3. For the Subject of Pledge — intellectual-property objects: insurance of the risk of loss of the exclusive right (subject to the business case).

3.4. For the Operator’s liability: - errors and omissions of the Operator’s employees that caused losses to DFA holders; - failures of the information system (cyber liability — within the agreed limit); - a personal-data leak and compensation under Federal Law No. 152-FZ.

4. Sum insured and insurance premium

4.1. The sum insured for the Subject of Pledge — not less than 100% of its appraised value.

4.2. The sum insured for the Operator’s liability — not less than ₽500 million (by analogy with the minimum limit for an investment-platform operator under 259-FZ).

4.3. The insurance premium is calculated under the Insurer’s fee schedule, taking into account the class of the property, the region, and the bonus-malus discount.

5. Insurance term

5.1. The insurance term coincides with the circulation period of the corresponding DFA with a 30-day “tail” after redemption.

6. Procedure of payments

6.1. The insurance payout is made to the Beneficiary within 30 days of recognising the event as insured.

6.2. In respect of the DFA — the payout goes to the Operator’s special distribution account, with subsequent distribution under the holders’ register.

7. Exclusions from insurance cover

7.1. Standard exclusions under Chapter 48 of the CC + the Insured’s wilful misconduct.

Status: framework draft, subject to adaptation for a specific insurer, a specific object and a risk package.

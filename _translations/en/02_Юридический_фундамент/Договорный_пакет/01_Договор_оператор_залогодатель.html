Operator–Pledgor Contract

Автор: Кагиров А.-Х.А. · Свидетельство о депонировании №4011265 от 19.12.2024 · Center Group Company · Engagement: CPFSR-RF-DESIGN-2026

“Operator – Pledgor” Contract (framework draft)

Subject: the conditions of the issuance of digital financial assets (DFAs) backed by the Pledgor’s property through the Digital Platform RSFSR (hereinafter — the Operator). Legal basis: arts. 334, 339, 339.1, 342, 345, 352 of the Civil Code of the RF; Federal Law No. 259-FZ of 31.07.2020 “On Digital Financial Assets…”; Federal Law No. 102-FZ of 16.07.1998 “On Mortgage (Real-Estate Pledge)”; Federal Law No. 353-FZ of 21.12.2013 “On Consumer Credit (Loan)” (applicable where the Pledgor is an individual).

1. Parties

1.1. The Operator — JSC “CP RSFSR”, a non-public joint-stock company, included in the register of operators of information systems within which DFAs are issued (CBR Directive No. 4336-U, 779-P), TIN/OGRN — [filled in upon signing].

1.2. The Pledgor — an individual or a legal entity transferring property as a pledge securing the performance of DFA obligations.

2. Subject of the contract

2.1. The Pledgor transfers to the Operator the right to encumber the property belonging to him (hereinafter — the “Subject of Pledge”), meeting the requirements of § 3 of this contract, for the purposes of issuing targeted infrastructure DFAs.

2.2. The Operator arranges: - the conduct of an independent appraisal of the Subject of Pledge (the contract with the appraiser is a separate document, § 02 of the contractual package); - insurance of the Subject of Pledge (insurance contract — § 03 of the contractual package); - registration of the encumbrance under the procedure established by law (USRRE — for real estate; the Pledge Notification Register — for movable property; Rospatent — for IP objects); - issuance of DFAs in the Operator’s information system under 259-FZ; - placement of the DFAs among qualified acquirers via a licensed exchange operator (in the pilot-light regime 2026–2028 — technological partnership with an existing operator of a DFA IS, including JSC “Atomyze”).

3. Requirements for the Subject of Pledge

3.1. Types of property accepted as pledge in the pilot-light regime: - real-estate objects (residential and non-residential) registered in the USRRE, free from other encumbrances; - vehicles registered with the State Traffic Inspectorate / GIMS / Rosreestr, not subject to a search; - intellectual property objects with registration at Rospatent; - issue-grade securities accounted for in the registers of professional securities-market participants.

3.2. The value of the Subject of Pledge is determined by an independent appraiser in accordance with Federal Law No. 135-FZ and FSO I–VII. The discount to the appraisal value (LTV) — not more than 70%.

4. Rights and obligations of the Parties

4.1. The Pledgor undertakes: - to provide reliable information on the Subject of Pledge and the rights thereto; - to ensure access for the appraiser and the insurer; - not to alienate the Subject of Pledge without the Operator’s consent during the DFA period; - to notify the Operator of any changes in the state of the Subject of Pledge.

4.2. The Pledgor has the right: - to receive remuneration under the issuance terms (coupon / distribution rate); - to early terminate the encumbrance upon full redemption of the DFA (art. 352 of the CC).

4.3. The Operator undertakes: - to arrange issuance, circulation and redemption of DFAs in compliance with 259-FZ; - to ensure the targeted spending of the funds raised through the escrow account under the VEB.RF rules (Project Finance Factory, Government Resolution No. 158); - to provide the Pledgor with quarterly reporting.

4.4. The Operator has the right: - to initiate realisation of the Subject of Pledge under the procedure established by law upon the occurrence of insured events and breach of the DFA issuance terms; - to receive commission remuneration under the Platform’s fee schedule.

5. Liability

5.1. The Parties bear liability in accordance with Chapter 25 of the CC of the RF and the provisions of the Federal Law “On the Protection of Consumer Rights” (for an individual Pledgor).

5.2. The Operator’s liability cannot be limited in violation of art. 16 of the Federal Law “On the Protection of Consumer Rights”.

6. Confidentiality and personal data

6.1. The Parties undertake to comply with Federal Law No. 152-FZ “On Personal Data”, Federal Law No. 98-FZ “On Commercial Secrets”. The Pledgor’s biometric data is processed exclusively through the UBS (CBT JSC) on the basis of 572-FZ and a separate written consent.

7. Term and procedure of termination

7.1. The contract is in force from the moment of DFA issuance until full performance of the obligations thereunder and removal of the encumbrance.

7.2. Early termination is possible under the procedure of arts. 352, 450, 451 of the CC.

8. Dispute resolution

8.1. Disputes are resolved in the Arbitration Court of Moscow (for legal entities) or in the court at the Pledgor’s place of residence (for individuals).

8.2. Mediation under Federal Law No. 193-FZ of 27.07.2010 is permitted.

Status: framework draft, subject to adaptation to the specifics of the transaction and to a legal review at the signing stage.

DFA-IS Operator Rules

Автор: Кагиров А.-Х.А. · Свидетельство о депонировании №4011265 от 19.12.2024 · Center Group Company · Engagement: CPFSR-RF-DESIGN-2026

Rules of the Operator of the DFA-Issuance Information System (framework draft)

Subject: the internal rules of the operator of the information system within which digital financial assets are issued, developed in accordance with art. 6 of Federal Law No. 259-FZ of 31.07.2020. Legal basis: art. 6 of Federal Law No. 259-FZ “On Digital Financial Assets…”; Bank of Russia Directive No. 4336-U of 25.11.2022; Bank of Russia Regulation No. 779-P “On the Requirements for the Activity of the Operator of an Information System…”.

1. General provisions

1.1. These Rules are an internal normative document of the operator JSC “CP RSFSR” (hereinafter — the Operator) and are mandatory for all users of the platform.

1.2. The Rules are published on the Operator’s official website in full-text access and in each User’s Personal Account.

1.3. Changes enter into force not earlier than 30 calendar days after publication.

2. Onboarding conditions (the User becomes a participant of the system)

2.1. The User undergoes identification and authentication procedures (UIAS, where necessary — UBS via CBT JSC under 572-FZ).

2.2. The User undergoes AML/CFT procedures in accordance with Federal Law No. 115-FZ and the Operator’s internal rules (CII-1, approved by order).

2.3. Conclusion with the Operator of an agreement of accession to these Rules.

2.4. Recognition of the User as a qualified investor (for the acquisition of the corresponding categories of DFAs) — under the rules of art. 63.4 of Federal Law No. 39-FZ “On the Securities Market”.

3. Conditions for DFA issuance

3.1. The Issuer files an application for the issuance of DFAs with attachment of: - the issue resolution (art. 4 of Federal Law No. 259-FZ); - documents on the Subject of Pledge and its appraisal; - documents on insurance; - the KYC document set of the Issuer.

3.2. The Operator conducts the expert review of the application within 10 business days.

3.3. After a positive decision is made — the DFA issuance is carried out with publication in the Operator’s register.

4. Conditions of DFA circulation

4.1. DFAs circulate exclusively among qualified acquirers until the corresponding category of DFAs receives publicly accessible status.

4.2. Transactions are recorded in the Operator’s information system with the time, price and parties fixed.

4.3. In the pilot-light 2026–2028 exchange operations are conducted via an existing licensed DFA exchange operator (technological partnership).

5. Conditions of redemption and termination of DFAs

5.1. Redemption of DFAs — on the grounds set in the issue resolution and in the smart contract.

5.2. Termination — upon full performance of the obligations, by agreement of the parties, by court decision.

6. Disclosure of information

6.1. The Operator discloses: - information on DFA issues (aggregated); - standardised quarterly reports; - information on material events.

6.2. The Issuer discloses: - information on the Subject of Pledge (aggregated); - information on insured events; - annual reports on the implementation of the project.

7. Handling of disputes and complaints

7.1. Internal procedure for handling Users’ complaints — within 15 business days.

7.2. Pre-trial procedure — mandatory mediation (where the dispute amount exceeds ₽1 million).

7.3. Judicial procedure — in accordance with arts. 32, 33 of the AC of the RF / arts. 28, 29 of the CCC of the RF.

8. Protection of personal data and commercial secret

8.1. Personal-data processing — in accordance with Federal Law No. 152-FZ, 572-FZ (biometrics via the UBS / CBT JSC).

8.2. Information classified as a commercial secret (Federal Law No. 98-FZ) is protected in the commercial-secret regime.

9. Operator’s liability

9.1. The Operator’s liability is determined in accordance with the legislation in force and cannot be limited in violation of art. 16 of the Law “On the Protection of Consumer Rights” (for Users — individuals).

9.2. The minimum amount of the Operator’s liability insurance — ₽500 million (see the insurance contract, § 03 of the package).

10. Final provisions

10.1. The Rules enter into force from the moment of publication on the Operator’s official website and registration in the register of DFA-IS operators.

10.2. Current version: a draft at the stage of preparation for inclusion in the Bank of Russia register (T+15…T+18 mo. from the launch of the corporate phase — the critical path of CIPF certification of 9–18 months).

Status: framework draft, subject to adaptation and to the full procedure of inclusion in the Bank of Russia register.

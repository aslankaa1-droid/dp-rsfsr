# CP RSFSR Contractual Package

**Purpose:** framework drafts of the key contracts required to launch the platform operator and to run the wedge-pilot 2026–2028. Closes AMBER A9 of the consolidated clarifying audit of 24.05.2026.

**Composition:**

| No. | Document | Subject | Legal basis |
|---|---|---|---|
| 01 | "Operator – Pledgor" contract | Issuance of DFAs against the Pledgor's property; encumbrance; rights/obligations; liability; insurance | Arts. 334, 339, 339.1, 342 of the CC of the RF; Federal Law No. 259-FZ; Federal Law No. 102-FZ |
| 02 | Contract with an independent appraiser | Appraisal of the subject of pledge under the FSO; liability; appraiser's insurance; SLA | Federal Law No. 135-FZ "On Appraisal Activity"; FSO I–VII |
| 03 | Insurance contract for the DFA and the subject of pledge | Insurance of the risk of loss/damage to the subject of pledge; insurance of the operator's liability | Law of the RF No. 4015-1; Chapter 48 of the CC of the RF |
| 04 | Escrow agent contract | Conditions for opening and servicing the escrow account; targeted spending of funds; control by VEB.RF/the Treasury; conditional disclosure | Arts. 860.7–860.10 of the CC of the RF; Federal Law No. 395-1; Government Resolution No. 158 (VEB.RF PFF) |
| 05 | Agreement "Rules of the operator of the DFA IS" | The operator's internal rules under 779-P: onboarding, deposit/withdrawal, dispute handling, disclosure | Art. 6 of Federal Law No. 259-FZ; CBR Directive No. 4336-U; 779-P |

**Status:** framework drafts. Full versions in the form of legally vetted standard templates are prepared at the stage of T+2…T+4 mo. jointly with engaged legal counsel (Pepeliaev Group / Bryan Cave Leighton Paisner Russia / SOB "Goltsblat-BLP"). Mandatory adaptation to the specifics of the transaction is required before signing with a particular counterparty.

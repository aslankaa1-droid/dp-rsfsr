# Escrow Agent Contract (framework draft)

**Subject:** the terms of opening and servicing the escrow account for targeted placement of the funds raised through the issuance of DFAs on the CP RSFSR platform.
**Legal basis:** arts. 860.7–860.10 of the Civil Code of the RF; Federal Law No. 395-1 of 02.12.1990 "On Banks and Banking Activity"; Government Resolution No. 158 of 15.02.2018 "On the Programme 'Project Finance Factory'" (for the wedge-pilot 2026–2028 with VEB.RF participation).

## 1. Parties

1.1. **The Depositor** — the DFA Issuer (the Pledgor / the Operator depending on the scheme).

1.2. **The Beneficiary** — the ultimate recipient of the targeted funds: the executor of a national / infrastructure project under the agreed target programme.

1.3. **The Escrow Agent** — a bank with a Bank of Russia licence under Federal Law No. 395-1 for banking operations and meeting the financial-soundness requirements. The escrow-account institute is regulated by arts. 860.7–860.10 of the CC of the RF; no special list of "authorised banks" is provided in 395-1 (such a list exists only for shared-equity construction under art. 15.5 of Federal Law No. 214-FZ). In the pilot-light 2026–2028 — agent banks of the VEB.RF Project Finance Factory (Gazprombank, PSB, Rosselkhozbank, in due course — Bank DOM.RF).

## 2. Purpose and subject

2.1. Purpose: to ensure the targeted use of the funds raised through DFAs, excluding the possibility of diverting funds to other purposes prior to the fulfilment of the conditions for disposing of the account.

2.2. The Escrow Agent opens the escrow account in the name of the Depositor and accepts the funds coming to it until the conditions specified in § 3 of this contract occur, for subsequent transfer to the Beneficiary.

## 3. Conditions for disposing of the account

3.1. The Escrow Agent transfers the funds to the Beneficiary only upon the simultaneous occurrence of:
- receipt of a Transfer Application signed by both parties with a breakdown of the targeted purpose;
- confirmation of the work / supply stage by an independent technical / financial project supervisor (for projects under the VEB.RF PFF — the VEB.RF methodology);
- the absence of prescriptions of a regulator (the Bank of Russia, the FTS, Rosfinmonitoring, the Treasury) suspending the transfer.

3.2. The targeted restrictions correspond to:
- the budget legislation of the RF where budget funds participate (the Budget Code of the RF);
- the conditions of a specific target programme / national project.

## 4. Control and reporting

4.1. The Escrow Agent provides the Parties weekly with an account statement and a register of operations.

4.2. The Escrow Agent notifies the Depositor and the Beneficiary of the balance of funds, receipts and upcoming transfers.

## 5. Disclosure of information to regulators

5.1. Upon a motivated request of the Bank of Russia (art. 4 of Federal Law No. 86-FZ), Rosfinmonitoring (Federal Law No. 115-FZ), the FTS (art. 86 of the Tax Code), the Escrow Agent discloses information on the movement of funds.

## 6. Term and procedure of termination

6.1. The contract is in force until the targeted purpose of the funds is fulfilled in full or until unused funds are returned to the Depositor (upon the occurrence of the conditions specified in the DFA issue).

## 7. Escrow Agent's remuneration

7.1. The amount of remuneration — under the Escrow Agent bank's fee schedule, agreed in a supplementary agreement.

## 8. Special conditions of the pilot-light involving VEB.RF

8.1. Where VEB.RF participates as the organiser of infrastructure financing through the PFF — operations on the escrow account are conducted in accordance with the PFF regulation and are accompanied by mandatory VEB.RF monitoring of the targeted use of funds.

8.2. The Bank of Russia in this construction is the general supervisory regulator of the financial market under art. 4 of Federal Law No. 86-FZ, without powers to approve the targeted purpose of the VEB.RF loan.

---

**Status:** framework draft, subject to adaptation to the chosen escrow agent bank and the specific target project.

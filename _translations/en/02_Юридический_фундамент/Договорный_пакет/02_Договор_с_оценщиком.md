# Contract with an Independent Appraiser (framework draft)

**Subject:** the provision of services for the appraisal of the Subject of Pledge and periodic revaluation for the purposes of DFA issuance on the CP RSFSR platform.
**Legal basis:** Federal Law No. 135-FZ of 29.07.1998 "On Appraisal Activity in the Russian Federation"; Federal Appraisal Standards (FSO) I–VII (orders of the Ministry of Economic Development); the Code of Ethics of the appraisers' self-regulatory organisation.

## 1. Parties

1.1. **The Client** — JSC "CP RSFSR" (the Operator) or the Pledgor directly (as agreed).

1.2. **The Appraiser** — an individual entrepreneur or a legal entity employing appraisers — members of an appraisers' self-regulatory organisation (SRO), with an effective mandatory liability-insurance contract under art. 24.7 of Federal Law No. 135-FZ.

## 2. Subject of the services

2.1. The Appraiser undertakes:
- to appraise the market (for individuals) or fair (IFRS 13 for legal entities) value of the Subject of Pledge using at least two approaches (FSO II);
- to prepare an Appraisal Report in accordance with the requirements of FSO V (Composition of the Report);
- to carry out revaluation at the frequency agreed with the Operator (as a rule — annually, and in crisis conditions — quarterly).

## 3. Appraiser's qualification

3.1. The Appraiser confirms:
- effective membership in an SRO (art. 22 of Federal Law No. 135-FZ);
- a qualification certificate in the relevant appraisal direction (art. 21.1 of Federal Law No. 135-FZ);
- insurance of the appraiser's liability for at least ₽30 million per insured event (exceeds the minimum of ₽300,000 under art. 24.7 of Federal Law No. 135-FZ);
- no conflict of interest with regard to the Operator and the Pledgor.

## 4. Liability

4.1. The Appraiser bears liability for the reliability of the Appraisal Report in accordance with art. 24.6 of Federal Law No. 135-FZ.

4.2. Liability insurance is a mandatory condition of the contract. The policy is attached as a separate annex.

4.3. The Appraiser reimburses the Client in full for losses arising from an inaccurate appraisal.

## 5. Confidentiality

5.1. All information about the Subject of Pledge, the Pledgor and the terms of the transaction is a commercial secret (Federal Law No. 98-FZ).

## 6. Cost and procedure of settlement

6.1. The cost of services is determined by the Appraiser's fee schedule agreed with the Operator. A fixed rate per object or a percentage of the appraised value (not more than 0.1%) is permitted.

6.2. Payment — 50% advance / 50% upon acceptance of the Report.

## 7. Term and procedure of termination

7.1. The contract is in force for one year, with automatic extension. Termination — 60 days' notice by either party.

---

**Status:** framework draft, subject to adaptation for the chosen appraiser and the actual object of appraisal.

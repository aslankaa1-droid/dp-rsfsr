# Letter of Intent (LoI · draft)

**Parties:**
- JSC "Atomyze" (hereinafter — the "Deal Operator"), operator of an information system for DFA issuance, included in the Bank of Russia register under Regulation No. 779-P.
- JSC "Digital Platform RSFSR" (under incorporation; hereinafter — the "Platform"), rights holder of the know-how under deposit certificate No. 4011265 of 19.12.2024 — Abdul-Khakim Akhmadovich Kagirov.

**Subject:** technological partnership in the category of "targeted infrastructure DFAs backed by property pledge" within the pilot-light 2026–2028 (Stage 1).

## 1. Architecture of cooperation

1.1. The Deal Operator carries out the issuance of DFAs in its information system on conditions agreed between the Parties and the Information System Rules (art. 6 of Federal Law No. 259-FZ).

1.2. The Platform acts as the front-end / agent for attracting pledgors, for the appraisal of the pledged property, for the registration of encumbrances in state registers (Rosreestr, GIBDD, depositories), for supporting smart contracts and for interaction with the settlement bank partner.

1.3. The Deal Operator's licence under Regulation No. 779-P **is personal and non-transferable.** The Platform does not claim a licence status in Stage 1.

## 2. Financing of placements

2.1. Settlements for issuances in Stage 1 are made in the cashless rouble or in the digital rouble (340-FZ) as a settlement complement.

2.2. Targeted infrastructure funding of placements is provided via the Project Finance Factory of VEB.RF (Government Resolution No. 158 of 15.02.2018) and the Federal Treasury within the framework of the Budget Code of the Russian Federation.

2.3. Additional emission by the Bank of Russia is not envisaged in Stage 1. The Bank of Russia is the general supervisory regulator under art. 4 of Federal Law No. 86-FZ.

## 3. Exclusivity

3.1. The Parties establish a **3-year exclusive partnership regime** in the category of "targeted infrastructure DFAs backed by the property of individuals and legal entities, tied to infrastructure funding from VEB.RF / the Federal Treasury".

3.2. Outside that category — both Parties are free in the choice of partners.

3.3. The exclusivity applies subject to reaching the target issuance volumes of ≥ ₽5 bn in Y1 and ≥ ₽20 bn in Y2.

## 4. Commercial terms (framework)

4.1. Deal Operator commission — 0.15% of the face value of each issuance (or another value as agreed).

4.2. Platform commission — 0.5% per issuance + 0.3% per annum of the outstanding stack (service fee).

4.3. The division of the service fee between the Parties is discussed separately depending on the volume of post-issuance support.

## 5. Timing

- Signing of the LoI: T+30 days from the incorporation of the JSC "Platform".
- Signing of the full MSA (Master Services Agreement): T+90 days.
- Launch of the first pilot DFA issuance: Q3 2027 (T+18 mo. from the incorporation of the JSC).

## 6. IP protection

6.1. The Platform retains exclusive rights to the know-how under certificate No. 4011265 of 19.12.2024. Atomyze receives a non-exclusive licence to use the know-how within the framework of this partnership; the licence period equals the exclusivity period (cl. 3.1).

6.2. Know-how reversion: upon termination of the partnership on any grounds, all rights revert to the rights holder unconditionally.

## 7. Confidentiality and applicable law

7.1. NDA — for 5 years after signing / termination.

7.2. Applicable law — the Russian Federation. Jurisdiction — the Arbitration Court of Moscow.

---

**Notice:** the LoI is of a framework nature and does not create an obligation to enter into an MSA. After the LoI is signed, the Parties carry out technical integration and fix the commercial terms in the MSA.

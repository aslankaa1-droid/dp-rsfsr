# Memorandum of Understanding (MoU · draft)

**Parties:**
- JSC "Gazprombank" / JSC "PSB" (hereinafter — the "Bank"), a systemically important credit institution.
- JSC "Digital Platform RSFSR" (under incorporation; hereinafter — the "Platform").

**Subject:** partnership of the Bank as the settlement and escrow agent in CP RSFSR projects.

## 1. Structure of the partnership

1.1. **Cash and settlement services** for the Platform and the operator JSC for the principal operations (DFA issuance, servicing of holders, payments of remuneration to pledge owners, swap).

1.2. **Escrow services** under arts. 860.7–860.10 of the Civil Code of the RF — for accumulating funds of targeted infrastructure financing with conditional deposit.

1.3. **Correspondent services** with operators of the digital rouble (Bank of Russia) for settlements in the form of the digital rouble (340-FZ).

1.4. **Financial expert assessment** of the pledge pool — for large facilities (≥ ₽100 mn face value of each DFA).

## 2. Volumes (Stage 1, pilot-light 2026–2028)

| Parameter | Y1 | Y2 | Y3 |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| Aggregate escrow turnover, ₽ bn | 5 | 30 | 100+ |
| Number of DFA issuances serviced | 10–20 | 100+ | 500+ |
| Bank's share in the Platform's total settlement turnover, % | 70 | 60 | 50 |

## 3. Commercial terms (framework)

3.1. **Bank escrow commission** — 0.05% per annum of the average annual balance on escrow accounts.

3.2. **Transfer commissions** — at the Bank's corporate-services tariffs with the possibility of individual agreement.

3.3. **Guaranteed volume** — the Platform undertakes to direct at least 60% of the settlement turnover through the Bank for the period of the MoU.

## 4. Timing

| Stage | Timing |
|---|---|
| T+0 | Signing of the MoU |
| T+60 days | Signing of the principal contracts (RCO, escrow, digital rouble) |
| T+90 days | Launch of the infrastructure and test settlements |
| T+120 days | First industrial DFA issuance with settlements through the Bank |

## 5. Key principles

5.1. **Transparency** — all operations are reflected in the DLT register of the Deal Operator and in the banking systems with audit capability.

5.2. **AML/KYC** — all Platform clients undergo the full identification procedure in accordance with 115-FZ; the results are available to the Bank.

5.3. **Cooling-off for large issuances** — for DFAs with a face value ≥ ₽1 bn the Bank has the right to additional expert assessment of the pledge (T+5 business days).

5.4. **Compliance with Bank of Russia Regulation No. 716-P** — operational risk and quality control of the infrastructure.

## 6. Protection

6.1. The Platform ensures full legal protection of the Bank's operations under 152-FZ, 115-FZ, 161-FZ.

6.2. The Bank has the right to unilaterally terminate the MoU upon identification of systematic violations of regulatory requirements by the Platform (cooling-off of 90 days).

6.3. NDA — for 5 years.

## 7. Applicable law

7.1. The Russian Federation. Arbitration — the Arbitration Court of Moscow.

---

**Notice:** the MoU is of a framework nature. Full contracts (RCO, escrow, correspondent) are signed after the operational details are agreed.

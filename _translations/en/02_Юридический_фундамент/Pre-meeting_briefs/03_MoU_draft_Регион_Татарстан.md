# Memorandum of Understanding (MoU · draft)

**Parties:**
- The Government of the Republic of Tatarstan (hereinafter — the "Region"), represented by the Head of the Republic or an authorised representative.
- JSC "Digital Platform RSFSR" (under incorporation; hereinafter — the "Platform").

**Subject:** the municipal pilot of CP RSFSR in the Republic of Tatarstan, 2026–2028.

## 1. Goals of the pilot

1.1. Demonstration of the two-stage wedge architecture of CP RSFSR on specific facilities of the regional infrastructure.

1.2. Attraction of off-budget financing of ₽5–10 bn in Y1, up to ₽20 bn by Y3, for targeted facilities:
- modernisation of the municipal utilities infrastructure;
- transport-purpose infrastructure projects (bridges, interchanges);
- projects in the sphere of healthcare and education;
- industrial parks and SEZs.

1.3. Formation of the first track record in the Russian Federation of targeted infrastructure DFAs backed by the property of the Region + private owners.

## 2. Pilot structure

| Parameter | Y1 (2026) | Y2 (2027) | Y3 (2028) |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| Number of facilities in the pilot | 10–20 | 30–50 | 50–100 |
| DFA issuance volume, ₽ bn | 5–10 | 15–20 | 20+ |
| Pledge pool (property of the Region + private) | municipal property; IZHS owners | + industrial parks | + large commercial assets |
| Partners | JSC "Atomyze" (779-P operator); VEB.RF PFF; bank partner | + Gazprombank / PSB; insurer | + second bank partner |

## 3. Roles of the Parties

3.1. **The Region:**
- provides the pledge pool of municipal property (≥ 30% of the Y1 volume);
- assists in attracting private owners (IZHS owners; regional enterprises);
- defines the priority directions of targeted use of the financing;
- ensures regulatory and administrative support.

3.2. **The Platform:**
- incorporates the operator JSC and enters into the technological partnership with Atomyze;
- arranges the DFA-issuance process (appraisal, encumbrance, placement, swap);
- attracts a bank partner (via the Ministry of Economic Development of the RF / the Ministry of Finance of the RF);
- arranges financing via the Project Finance Factory of VEB.RF (Government Resolution No. 158);
- ensures technological integration with the State IS.

## 5. Key principles

5.1. **No transfer of property.** The pledged property remains in the ownership of the Region / private owners throughout the DFA period; only a temporary encumbrance is set.

5.2. **Targeted use** — strictly for infrastructure projects agreed with the Region, through the Federal Treasury and accounts under arts. 860.7–860.10 of the Civil Code.

5.3. **Transparency** — all operations are recorded in the DLT register of the IS of the Deal Operator (Atomyze).

5.4. **Additional income to the owners** — 1–2% per annum of the DFA face value (agreed separately for each issuance).

5.5. **Force-majeure protection of the Region** — mandatory insurance of the pledged property (Law of the RF No. 4015-1, arts. 32, 32.9).

## 6. Confidentiality and applicable law

6.1. NDA — for 5 years.

6.2. Applicable law — the Russian Federation. Arbitration — the Arbitration Court of the Republic of Tatarstan.

# Consultative-Letter Request (draft)

**Addressee:** State Development Corporation "VEB.RF", Project Finance Factory.

**From:** JSC "Digital Platform RSFSR" (under incorporation).

**Subject:** request as to the applicability of Government Resolution No. 158 of 15.02.2018 "On the Programme 'Project Finance Factory'" to CP RSFSR projects.

## 1. Brief project description

CP RSFSR is a front-end / agency platform for the issuance of DFAs (Federal Law No. 259-FZ of 31.07.2020) backed by the property of individuals and legal entities. Placements are conducted through technological partnership with an active operator of a DFA-issuance information system under Bank of Russia Regulation No. 779-P (JSC "Atomyze" or another subject of the register). Support of the DFA life cycle: appraisal of the pledge (Federal Law No. 135-FZ), registration of encumbrances (218-FZ, arts. 339.1, 358.1 of the CC), smart contract, DFA swap to the built facility.

## 2. Target sectors of financing

DFAs are issued for targeted infrastructure projects in the categories:
- transport infrastructure (bridges, roads, logistics hubs);
- public-utilities modernisation;
- healthcare (infrastructure modernisation, equipment);
- science and education;
- import substitution in critical industries.

These categories coincide with the priorities of the Project Finance Factory under item 5 of Government Resolution No. 158.

## 4. Request

We ask VEB.RF PFF to:

4.1. Confirm the **principal applicability** of the instruments of the Project Finance Factory (Government Resolution No. 158) to projects attracted through CP RSFSR as a front-end channel.

4.2. Define the **methodology for assessing the eligibility of CP RSFSR projects** for inclusion in the PFF portfolio.

4.3. Agree on a **framework regime of interaction** between JSC "CP RSFSR" and VEB.RF PFF at the pilot-placements stage (Stage 1).

4.4. Appoint a **profile coordinator on the PFF side** for coordinating the technological integration and procedural matters.

#### Cash-runway stress test

- 2025–2026 cumulative FCF = RUB −2,080 mn.
- A USD 15M round (RUB 1,335 mn) closes 64% of the peak.

## Methodological notes (updated post-audit)

1. **WACC** = build-up without double-CRP in RUB-denominated RF; β = 1.15 (Damodaran fintech 2026); ERP RF 9% (Damodaran May 2026). Resulting 30% is a defensible minimum.
2. **Multi-stage TV** has replaced Gordon — the TV share in NPV has dropped from 49% to 9%.
3. **TV EBITDA margin** = 74.8% (vs 96.9%) — realistic given OpEx growth with scale.
4. **Equity Multiple** 12× (exit-on-multiple) — within the upper band of tier-1 fintech.
5. **Scenarios** have explicit weights (15/55/30) and Expected Value of USD 88M.
6. **Sensitivities** have been extended: added OpEx × timing, TV EBITDA margin × g, Monte Carlo, cash-runway stress, break-even.
7. **FX** is fixed: RUB/USD 89.0 as of 24.05.2026.
8. **CBR key rate** is fixed: 21.0% as of 24.05.2026.

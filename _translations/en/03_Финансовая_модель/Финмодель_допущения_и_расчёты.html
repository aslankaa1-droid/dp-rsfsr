CPFSR Financial Model — notes on assumptions and calculations

Document: accompanying note to the financial model. Related file: Финансовая_модель_ЦП_РСФСР.md (horizon 2025–2034 + multi-stage TV to 2045+). Concept author: A.-Kh. A. Kagirov, deposit certificate No. 4011265 of 19.12.2024 — fixation of the priority date and the hash; protection regime — know-how (Article 1465 CC) + software (Article 1262 CC). Reference date: 24.05.2026 (refactor following the composite audit; single SoT — _Мастер/Единые_показатели_v2.md).

The purpose is to disclose the source of each figure and identify constraints, so that any tier-1 DD auditor can walk through the model with full understanding of the logic.

1. Market and platform share

1.1. Collateral capacity RUB 25 tn

Source: author’s concept (РСФСР_6.pptx, slide 9).

Conservatism logic: only 10% of assets are engaged in the DFA system over a 10-year horizon, and the appraisal value for collateral purposes is 50% of market value (consistent with Basel LTV practice).

1.2. Market indexation of 4% p.a.

The principal directional indicator of the Bank of Russia monetary policy.

1.4. DFA stack (outstanding volume)

Formula: stack(t) = sum of issuances over years (t−4)…t. Average DFA life = 5 years (infrastructure cycles).

5. Payroll

Headcount 40→370; average gross salary RUB 350k/month in Year 1, indexation 6% p.a.; social contributions 30%.

6. Taxes

  • Profit tax 20% (Tax Code, Chapter 25). With IT accreditation + ≥ 70% IT revenue → 5% until 31.12.2030 (separate scenario: +25–30% to NPV).
  • VAT: DFA transactions — sub-para 12.2 of para 2 of Article 149 of the Tax Code (exemption); operator services — general rate of 20%.
  • Social contributions 30% (Tax Code, Chapter 34).

7. WACC (refactor per audit C5)

Adjustment: removal of CRP double-counting in RUB-denominated RF; ERP raised from 6% to 9% (Damodaran May 2026); β set at 1.15.

Component Value
Risk-free rate (10Y OFZ, 24.05.2026) 15.5%
ERP × β (RF May 2026, β = 1.15) 9% × 1.15 = 10.35%
Stage premium (MVP ready) 1.5%
Liquidity premium 2.5%
WACC (RUB) total 29.85% → 30.0%

Alternative methods: - CAPM (RUB): 15.5 + 1.15 × 9 = 25.85% — lower bound of the range. - Build-up: 15.5 + 6 + 2.5 + 3 = 27% — inside the range. - CBR rate + spread: 21% + 9 = 30% — matches.

Defensible range: 27–32%. The Base case uses 30%.

8. Multi-stage TV (refactor per audit C4)

Replacement of the single-stage Gordon TV with a 3-stage model with an explicit fade phase for growth rate and EBITDA margin:

Phase Years g EBITDA margin
I — maturity 2035–2039 6% → 4% 75% → 70%
II — stability 2040–2044 3.5% → 2.5% 70% → 65%
III — perpetuity 2045+ 2.0% (½ of CBR target) 60%

TV share in full NPV = 9.0% (was 49%) — the critical threshold has been removed.

9. Sensitivities (extended set)

Added per audit C9–C11: - WACC × Market share (5×5, existing). - Commissions × Inflation (3×5, existing). - OpEx curve × Launch timing (5×3, new). - FTE × Average salary (5×5, new). - TV EBITDA margin × g (3×3, new). - Tornado diagram top-10. - Simplified Monte Carlo (1,000 iterations, NPV median = RUB 91 bn, P10–P90 = 38–162). - Break-even analysis (revenue at NPV = 0: RUB 22.8 bn in 2030). - Cash-runway stress test (a +6-month delay → +RUB 0.38 bn cash gap; recommendation — USD 20M round).

10. Working capital

5% of revenue — a standard assumption for fintech operators (Russian payment systems: 3–7%, depositories: 5–10%).

11. What is not accounted for in the base model (and why)

  1. Government subsidies (RFRIT, Ministry of Digital Development) — upside, not included.
  2. Debt financing — the model is equity-only; a mixed structure would deliver an additional tax shield.
  3. FX risk — all flows are in rubles; no FX exposure.
  4. Competition for collateral — Base assumes a unique category over the first 5 years; covered in the Conservative scenario.
  5. Regulatory risk of rejection — disclosed in 04_Бизнес-план/Wedge_pilot-light.md: should the legislative package not be adopted, the project operates in pilot-light mode under the existing 259-FZ (Conservative scenario — NPV RUB 41 bn).

12. Consistency with the interactive finmodel.js

After audit point C9, synchronisation of _shared/finmodel.js v2 ↔︎ the MD model is achieved: payroll, CapEx waves, working capital, depreciation, tax shield and the multi-stage TV are all accounted for. The calculator in the major presentation produces NPV/IRR identical to the calculations in this document at the same slider settings.

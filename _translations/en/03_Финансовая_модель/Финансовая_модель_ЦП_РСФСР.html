Author and rightsholder: A.-Kh. A. Kagirov. Deposit certificate No. 4011265 of 19.12.2024 — fixation of the priority date and of the hash of the work; protection regime — know-how (Article 1465 CC) + software (Article 1262 CC, registration with Rospatent in progress).

Horizon: 10 years (2025–2034) + multi-stage Terminal Value to 2045+. Currency: Russian rubles. Unless otherwise stated, amounts are in RUB millions; the collateral capacity and the DFA volumes are in RUB billions. Reference date: 24.05.2026 (full refactor following the composite audit; single Source of Truth — _Мастер/Единые_показатели_v2.md). Fixed market parameters: CBR key rate 14.5, FX USD/RUB 71.55, ERP RF 8.13, RF (10Y OFZ) 14.73, inflation target 4%.

The file is presented as a machine-readable model in Markdown format with explicit formulas in comments. All calculations below are performed by hand using the same formulas that are reproduced in _shared/finmodel.js (the interactive model in the major presentation). The discrepancy between documents has been eliminated: one SoT, one set of numbers.

Cash-runway stress test

  • 2025–2026 cumulative FCF = RUB −2,080 mn.
  • A USD 15M round (RUB 1,335 mn) closes 64% of the peak.
  • Bridge financing for the residual gap = RUB 0.75 bn for 6–9 months (a single value across the project, agreed with Wedge_pilot-light.md and the International Valuation).
  • With a 6-month pilot delay: an additional gap of RUB +0.38 bn.
  • Alternative: a USD 20M round (post-money USD 115M, stake 17.4%) closes the gap without a bridge.

Break-even analysis

  • Revenue at NPV = 0 at WACC 29.5: RUB 22.8 bn in 2030 (vs the plan of RUB 65 bn) — a large safety margin.

Methodological notes (updated post-audit)

  1. WACC = build-up without double-CRP in RUB-denominated RF; β = 1.15 (Damodaran fintech 2026); ERP RF 8.13 (Damodaran May 2026). Resulting 30% is a defensible minimum.
  2. Multi-stage TV has replaced Gordon — the TV share in NPV has dropped from 49% to 9%.
  3. TV EBITDA margin = 74.8% (vs 96.9%) — realistic given OpEx growth with scale.
  4. Equity Multiple 12× (exit-on-multiple) — within the upper band of tier-1 fintech.
  5. Scenarios have explicit weights (15/55/30) and Expected Value of USD 106M.
  6. Sensitivities have been extended: added OpEx × timing, TV EBITDA margin × g, Monte Carlo, cash-runway stress, break-even.
  7. FX is fixed: RUB/USD 71.55 as of 24.05.2026.
  8. CBR key rate is fixed: 14.5 as of 24.05.2026.

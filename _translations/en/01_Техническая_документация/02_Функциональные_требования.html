Functional Requirements (PRD) of CPFSR

The document describes the functional modules of the platform and user scenarios in the User Story + acceptance criteria format.

1. List of functional modules

ID Module Purpose
M-01 Mortgagor’s personal account Registration, KYC, profile, assets, income, documents.
M-02 Registration and identification (KYC/AML) Via ESIA, EBS; AML check under FZ-115; risk scoring.
M-03 Asset catalogue Submission of assets for collateral; integration with EGRN / Rospatent / registers.
M-04 Valuation module Request to an authorised valuer; valuation result; insurance.
M-05 DFA Issuer Issue, accounting, status transitions, exchange of DFAs in the DLT register.
M-06 Smart contracts Designer, templates, execution, events.
M-07 CBR escrow adapter Transfer of DFAs to the CBR; integration with the special escrow.
M-08 Federal Treasury adapter Confirmation of targeted expenditure.
M-09 Portfolio management Analysis of the operator’s positions, analytics, forecasts.
M-10 Regulatory reporting Generation of forms for the CBR, Rosfinmonitoring, FNS.
M-11 Partner cabinet Cabinets of valuers, insurers, partner banks.
M-12 Administrative cabinet Management of users, roles, policies, audit log.
M-13 Security and audit DLP, SIEM integration, immutable event log.
M-14 Notifications Push, SMS, email, notifications via Gosuslugi.

2. Key user scenarios

US-01. Registration of a mortgagor

As the owner of property I want to register on the platform via Gosuslugi in order to gain access to submitting assets as collateral.

Acceptance criteria: 1. Authorisation via ESIA with a confirmed account. 2. Verification against the register of disqualified persons and sanction lists (FZ-115). 3. Verification of personal data for correctness. 4. On success — issuance of a personal platform ID (usr-rsfsr-{n}). 5. In case of non-compliance — a reasoned refusal with the cause specified.

US-02. Submission of an asset as collateral

As a mortgagor I want to submit an asset with automatic loading of characteristics from EGRN/Rospatent in order not to enter data manually.

Acceptance criteria: 1. Property type selected from the directory (real estate, vehicle, intellectual property, securities). 2. For real estate — auto-loading from EGRN by cadastral number. 3. For vehicles — from GIS GIBDD (where applicable). 4. For IP — from Rospatent. 5. For securities — from the depositary register. 6. Possibility of manual completion. 7. Attachment of documents (certificate, contracts). 8. Request for valuation to the partner valuation organisation.

US-03. Asset valuation

As a partner valuer I want to receive a valuation request, perform the valuation and record the result in order to trigger the DFA issue.

Acceptance criteria: 1. The valuer receives the request in the partner cabinet. 2. The valuation is carried out under the methodology (FZ-135). 3. The result is recorded in a form with mandatory fields: appraisal value, method, date, insurance. 4. Electronic signature of the result by the valuer (UKEP). 5. Automatic publication of the result in the mortgagor’s cabinet. 6. Validity period of the valuation — 6 months (standard).

US-04. Signing of a smart contract

As a mortgagor I want to configure the conditions and sign the DFA smart contract in order to trigger the asset issue.

Acceptance criteria: 1. Conditions designer: term (12/24/36/60 months), share of par value (default 50% of the appraisal), reward rate, target use. 2. Display of the full text of the smart contract (PDF preview). 3. Two-factor confirmation (ESIA + SMS/biometrics). 4. Signing with a UKEP under GOST R 34.10-2012. 5. After signing — automatic registration of the encumbrance in EGRN. 6. Notification to the mortgagor on completion.

US-05. DFA issue

As the platform operator I want to automatically issue a DFA after the signing of the smart contract and the encumbrance in order to transfer it to the Bank of Russia.

Acceptance criteria: 1. Creation of a record in the DLT register with a unique ID (cfa-{hash}-rsfsr-{year}-{seq}). 2. Hashing of the smart contract under GOST R 34.11-2012. 3. Notification to the Bank of Russia via a secured channel. 4. Confirmation from the Bank of Russia on the receipt of the DFA. 5. Return of the confirmation to the mortgagor.

US-06. Exchange of DFAs

As the operator I want to automatically perform an exchange of DFAs after the commissioning of a new facility in order to substitute the owner’s initial collateral.

Acceptance criteria: 1. Receipt of the title certificate for the new facility via EGRN. 2. Issue of a new DFA with a larger appraisal. 3. Exchange request to the Bank of Russia. 4. Confirmation of the exchange, signing of the Mutual Settlement Act by all parties. 5. Removal of the encumbrance in EGRN. 6. Payment of remuneration to the owner to the bank account.

US-07. Insurance event

As the operator I want to initiate an insurance DFA from the partner insurance company in order to compensate the owner’s initial DFA.

Acceptance criteria: 1. Registration of the insurance event with documentary confirmation. 2. Issue by the insurance company of its own DFA through the platform. 3. Exchange with the owner’s initial DFA. 4. Signing of the act. 5. Payment of compensation.

US-08. Income and history

As a mortgagor I want to see the history of my DFAs and income in order to manage the portfolio.

Acceptance criteria: 1. Dashboard with assets, statuses, current income. 2. Payment history. 3. Export to PDF and Excel. 4. Tax statement 2-NDFL automatically (for individuals).

US-09. Regulatory reporting

As the operator I want to generate mandatory reporting for regulators in order to comply with the requirements of FZ-259 and CBR instructions.

Acceptance criteria: 1. Generation of FZ-115 reporting for Rosfinmonitoring. 2. Regular reports to the CBR (under 779-P). 3. Tax reporting on DFA operations. 4. Approval of UKEP signatures.

US-10. Information security incident management

As an information security officer I want to receive events from SIEM in order to respond to incidents under Instruction No. 6635-U.

Acceptance criteria: 1. Categorisation of incidents. 2. Notification to the Bank of Russia within the established timeframes. 3. Notification to GosSOPKA. 4. Response journal.

3. Business rules

  1. Collateral value = not more than 50% of the appraisal value (a conservative rule for the pilot phase).
  2. Minimum DFA term = 12 months. Maximum = 60 months.
  3. Reward rate to the mortgagor = from 3% to 6% per annum (determined by the conditions of the national project).
  4. One natural person may simultaneously hold no more than 5 active DFAs.
  5. One legal entity — no more than 50 active DFAs.
  6. Insurance of the collateral asset and the DFA itself is mandatory for all DFAs above RUB 1 million.
  7. Prohibition of double collateral: one asset may not simultaneously be encumbered to CPFSR and to any other organisation.
  8. Prohibition of DFA circulation by the Bank of Russia (under the author’s concept, item 10 of the step-by-step process).

4. DFA states

DRAFT → PENDING_VALUATION → VALUED → CONTRACT_SIGNED →
ISSUED → TRANSFERRED_TO_CB → ACTIVE →
[REINVESTED | RETURNED | INSURED_CASE | MENA_DONE]

Each transition is an event in the DLT register, immutable.

# Expert Opinion on the project documentation
## Digital Platform CPFSR

**Purpose of the document:** an independent expert review of the complete CPFSR project artefact pack for consistency with the author's concept, the current legislation of the Russian Federation, regulators' requirements and international standards of project documentation quality.
**Approach:** component-by-component review of each artefact against a check-list of five quality criteria: (1) consistency with the original concept; (2) completeness; (3) legal accuracy; (4) technical justification; (5) coherence with other documents in the pack.
**Rating scale:** 5 — excellent compliance, no remarks; 4 — compliance with minor remarks; 3 — acceptable subject to revision; 2 — material gaps; 1 — non-compliant.

## 8. Final expert conclusion

The Digital Platform CPFSR project documentation pack **exceeds the client's requirements and the standard level of project documentation for fintech start-ups in the Russian Federation**. It can be presented for discussion with regulators, investors, state customers and project partners.

**Strengths:**
1. The idea is unique and protected by know-how deposit.
2. The legal framework is developed with references to specific articles of federal laws and CBR Regulations.
3. The financial model is sound and resilient under sensitivity analysis.
4. The technology stack is realistic and import-independent.
5. The design system is ready for industrial use.
6. **A full-fledged mobile application has been developed and is ready for publication**.
7. **A complete GOST 19 design-documentation pack is ready for submission to the Ministry of Digital Development register and FSTEC certification**.
8. **Platform builds for Android / iOS / Windows are ready for compilation via Capacitor**.
9. **The RuStore publication pack is fully assembled**.
10. Localisation in 4 languages (including Arabic with RTL) extends the target audience.
11. All figures are reconciled between documents.
12. No speculation beyond the source materials.
13. WCAG 2.2 AA accessibility across all artefacts.

**Areas for next-stage work (priority — pre-pilot):**
1. Obtain Ministry of Finance and FNS clarifications on the taxation of DFA exchange transactions.
2. Run a PoC of two DLT providers and select a vendor.
3. Sign memoranda of intent with 2–3 banks and 2–3 insurance companies.
4. Prepare a quarterly cash-flow model for the first 24 months.
5. Prepare the legislative drafts as formal proposals for submission.
6. Publish the mobile application on RuStore (after registration of the operator legal entity).
7. Apply for FSTEC / FSB certification.
8. Apply for inclusion in the Ministry of Digital Development register (Decree No. 1236).

**Expert recommendation:** the pack is ready for negotiations with the Bank of Russia, investors and state customers. The quality of the documents matches the international level of the Big Four / major Russian consultancies (Technologies of Trust / B1 / Kept / BDO / RSM). The readiness of the client side (mobile application + DD + builds + RuStore) raises the market valuation of the project from the base zone of USD 60M to **USD 80M pre-money**, consistent with Series A logic with product investment already made.

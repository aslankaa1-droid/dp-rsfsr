## Full description (up to 4,000 characters)

**CPFSR** is an information mobile application of the Digital Platform "Russian Service for Social Development Financing". The application introduces the concept of operation of the future operator for the issue of targeted digital financial assets against property collateral and collects community feedback on use scenarios.

### What the application is about

The application explains how, according to the author's concept (deposit certificate No. 4011265 of 19.12.2024), the platform for the issue of targeted DFAs against the collateral of citizens' and organisations' property may be arranged. A fully functional operator may be launched only after:
- establishment of a corporate legal entity in the form of a non-public joint-stock company (Art. 5 of FZ-259);
- inclusion of this entity in the Bank of Russia DFA IS operators register (Regulation 779-P);
- registration as a personal data operator with Roskomnadzor (Art. 22 of FZ-152);
- registration with Rosfinmonitoring (internal control rules under FZ-115);
- obtaining the relevant approvals of FSTEC / FSB.

Until the above steps are completed, the application operates exclusively as an information and demonstration service.

### Concept of operation (in demo mode)

In the concept, the owner submits an object owned by them (a flat, a house, a land plot, a vehicle, intellectual property, securities) as collateral. The property **remains in the owner's ownership** — only a temporary encumbrance in favour of the platform is imposed on it.

The platform issues a targeted digital financial asset (DFA) based on the asset. On the horizon of the adoption of the relevant package of federal laws (T+36 … T+60 months), targeted funding through the VEB.RF / Treasury infrastructure channel with the Bank of Russia's participation as the supervisory regulator is envisaged against the pool of targeted DFAs. Until the regulatory acts package is adopted, the operator works in pilot-light mode under the current FZ-259 — without targeted funding and without operations requiring amendments to FZ-86.

In the concept, the asset owner receives a regular reward for the entire time the DFA is used as a collateral instrument. Upon expiry of the term, the encumbrance is removed and the property is returned to full disposal.

### What is inside the application (demo mode)

— Viewing the architecture of the future service and use scenarios.
— Emulated authorisation via Gosuslugi (ESIA) for scenario testing — the user's credentials are not transmitted or stored.
— Demo asset submission pages with automatic loading of illustrative data (not from the real EGRN).
— Demo pages for the valuation request to an accredited valuation company.
— Illustration of a working smart contract.
— Demonstration of the "portfolio" view — on illustrative data.
— Illustration of tax statements.

### Security

The architecture complies with the strict requirements of Russian regulators:
— Critical information infrastructure object category — 1st (highest) after categorisation.
— Protection level under GOST R 57580.1 — 4th (maximum).
— Personal data security level — UZ-1.
— Cryptography: GOST R 34.10-2012, GOST R 34.11-2012, GOST R 34.12-2015.
— Compliance with FZ-152 (personal data protection), FZ-187 (CII), FZ-115 (AML/CTF).

In demo mode, real personal data of users are not collected.

### Legal basis of the concept

The concept is built with reference to:
— Federal Law No. 259-FZ of 31.07.2020 "On Digital Financial Assets" (current acting regulation).
— Federal Law No. 86-FZ of 10.07.2002 "On the Central Bank of the Russian Federation" (horizon package of amendments T+36 … T+60 months).
— Federal Law No. 340-FZ of 24.07.2023 (digital rouble).
— Bank of Russia Regulations No. 779-P, No. 683-P, No. 716-P, No. 499-P.

All wordings relating to targeted funding through the Bank of Russia correctly refer to the **future legal regime** that requires the adoption of a federal-law package. Until the adoption of the relevant regulatory acts, the corresponding functions in the application are presented in an exclusively illustrative form.

## Limitations and reservations

— The application is an **information-demonstration service** and does not provide licensed financial services.
— Asset submission, valuation request, smart contract, payments — illustrative, do not result in legally significant actions.
— Authorisation via ESIA — in demo mode without transmission to the real Gosuslugi contour.
— Biometric confirmation (EBS) — an illustrative function; real biometric data are not collected and are not transmitted to EBS.
— No promises of income, payments, yield or emission by the Bank of Russia are made in the application.
— A DFA is not a cryptocurrency. The application has no relation to unbacked digital currencies.

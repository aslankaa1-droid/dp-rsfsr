## 1. General provisions

This Privacy Policy (hereinafter — the "Policy") sets out the procedure for processing personal data of users of the "CPFSR" mobile application (hereinafter — the "Application") in accordance with:

- Federal Law No. 152-FZ of 27.07.2006 "On Personal Data".
- Federal Law No. 572-FZ of 29.12.2022 "On Identification and (or) Authentication of Individuals Using Biometric Personal Data" — the operator of the Unified Biometric System (EBS) is JSC "Centre for Biometric Technologies" (JSC CBT).
- Resolution of the Government of the Russian Federation No. 1119 of 01.11.2012 (security levels).
- FSTEC of Russia Order No. 21.

Use of the Application constitutes consent to this Policy regarding the processing of identification, contact, technical and asset-related personal data (categories 2.1, 2.2, 2.4, 2.5 below). **Consent to the processing of biometric personal data (category 2.3) is given by a separate legally significant action** — the "I consent to the processing of biometric data" button with the fact of consent recorded and linked to a specific version of the Policy (part 1, part 4 of Art. 9 of FZ-152; Arts. 8, 14 of FZ-572). Without a separate consent, the biometric functions of the Application are unavailable. Consent can be revoked at any time in the "Settings → Privacy → Biometrics" section.

> **Status of data processing at the time of publication.** The actual processing of users' personal data begins only after the Operator submits a notification to Roskomnadzor under Art. 22 of FZ-152 (deadline — no less than 30 days before the start of processing). Until the registration in the Roskomnadzor personal data operators register is completed, the Application operates in demo mode exclusively on test scenarios; the personal data of real users are not collected or processed.

## 2. What data we process

### 2.1. Identification data
- Surname, first name, patronymic.
- Date of birth.
- Series and number of the identity document.
- INN.
- SNILS.
- ID of the Gosuslugi (ESIA) account.

### 2.2. Contact data
- Mobile phone number.
- Email address.
- Region of residence.

### 2.3. Biometric data
- Biometric identifiers from the Unified Biometric System — operator JSC "Centre for Biometric Technologies" (JSC CBT) under FZ-572.
- The Application **does not collect, store or process biometric vectors** locally. Only the verification fact performed on the EBS side is used, under the technical protocol of FZ-572.
- The processing of biometrics is possible exclusively with separate explicit user consent (see § 1 above; part 1, part 4 of Art. 9 of FZ-152).

### 2.4. Asset data
- Information about collateral objects (cadastral number, address, area, value).
- Title deed documents.
- Valuation results.
- Parameters of issued DFAs.
- Payment history.

### 2.5. Technical data
- Unique device identifier.
- IP address.
- Operating system and its version.
- Device model.
- Time and place (geolocation — only with explicit consent) of use.
- Logs of actions in the Application.

## 3. Purposes of processing

1. Identification and authentication of the user.
2. Passing the KYC procedure in accordance with FZ-115.
3. Submission of an asset as collateral.
4. Issue and servicing of DFAs.
5. Calculation and payment of remuneration.
6. Notification of key smart-contract events.
7. Regulatory reporting to the Bank of Russia, Rosfinmonitoring, FNS.
8. Information security assurance.
9. User support.
10. Compliance with statutory requirements.

## 4. Legal grounds for processing

- Consent of the user (item 1 of part 1 of Art. 6 of FZ-152).
- Service contract (item 5 of part 1 of Art. 6 of FZ-152).
- Performance of functions imposed by the legislation of the Russian Federation (item 2 of part 1 of Art. 6 of FZ-152).
- Achievement of socially significant goals (item 7 of part 1 of Art. 6 of FZ-152).

## 5. Processing periods

- During active use of the Application.
- For 5 years after account deletion — data required for the performance of obligations to regulators.
- For 10 years — audit logs (requirement of FZ-187 and Bank of Russia instructions).
- Indefinitely — records of issued DFAs in the distributed register (marked with deletion of the owner's personal data on request).

## 6. To whom we transfer data

### 6.1. To state bodies
- Bank of Russia (for regulatory oversight).
- Federal Treasury (for targeted financing).
- Rosfinmonitoring (for mandatory control under FZ-115).
- FNS of Russia (for tax accounting).
- Rosreestr (for the registration of encumbrances).
- Law enforcement agencies (under an official request in the statutory manner).

### 6.2. To operation partners
- Partner valuation organisations (only for the valuation of a specific asset).
- Partner insurance organisations (only for the arrangement of insurance).
- Partner credit organisations (for payments to a bank account).

### 6.3. To service providers
- Cloud infrastructure providers on the territory of the Russian Federation.
- Security system providers (SIEM, EDR) — without access to personal data in clear form.

**Transfer of data outside the Russian Federation is not carried out** in line with item 5 of Art. 18 of FZ-152.

## 7. Data protection measures

- CII object category: 1st (highest).
- ISPDn security level: UZ-1.
- Protection level under GOST R 57580.1: 4th.
- Cryptography: GOST R 34.10-2012, GOST R 34.11-2012.
- Local storage of sensitive data — only in the protected storages of the operating system.
- Encryption of data in transit — TLS with GOST certificates.
- Regular IS audit — annually.
- External pentest — annually.

## 8. Your rights

In accordance with Arts. 14, 15, 16, 18 of FZ-152, you have the right to:

- Receive confirmation of the fact of the processing of your personal data.
- Receive information about the personal data processed.
- Demand clarification, blocking, destruction of inaccurate or unlawfully processed personal data.
- Revoke consent to the processing of personal data.
- Appeal the Operator's actions to Roskomnadzor and in court.

### How to exercise rights
1. Through the "Profile → Security" section in the Application.
2. By email: privacy@dp-rsfsr.ru.
3. By a written application to the Operator's address (to be specified after the registration of the legal entity).

## 9. Cookies and similar technologies

The Application uses local storages (LocalStorage, IndexedDB) for:
- Saving interface settings (theme, language, font size).
- Caching data for offline mode.
- Saving session identifiers.

**Third-party trackers and advertising networks are not used by the Application.**

## 10. Push notifications

Push notifications are sent:
- About key smart-contract events (DFA exchange, payments, insurance events).
- About new financing programmes.
- About technical works.

You can disable push notifications in the "Settings" section of the Application or by means of the operating system.

## 11. Minors

The Application is intended for persons over 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect minors' data. Upon detection of such data, it is immediately deleted.

## 12. Changes to the Policy

We may amend the Policy. Notice of changes is published in the Application and on the dp-rsfsr.ru website 30 days before the changes take effect. Use of the Application after the changes take effect constitutes consent to the new version.

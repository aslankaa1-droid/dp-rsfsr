# Application description

## CPFSR — mobile application

**Identifier:** РСФСР.МП.03.ОПР
**Standard:** GOST 19.502-78

---

## 1. Programme purpose

### 1.1. Capabilities
The application implements the client functionality of the Digital Platform CPFSR for asset owners:
- registration and identification;
- submission of property as collateral;
- issue and servicing of digital financial assets (DFAs);
- receipt of income from placement;
- management of the portfolio of assets and DFAs.

### 1.2. Main characteristics
- 23 user screens.
- 4 themes (light, dark, soft, high-contrast A11Y).
- 4 interface languages (Russian, English, French, Arabic with RTL).
- Full WCAG 2.2 AA accessibility.
- Offline mode via Service Worker.
- Support for installation as a native application on iOS, Android, Windows.
- PWA mode support for any modern browser.

### 1.3. Information on limitations
- Full-fledged operations (DFA issue, signing) require a network connection.
- Confirmation of operations — through ESIA and EBS.
- Import-independent cryptography (GOST R 34.10-2012 / 34.11-2012 / 34.12-2015).

## 2. Conditions of use

### 2.1. Conditions required for the programme execution

#### Technical conditions
- iOS 15+ / Android 9+ / Windows 10+ / a modern browser with Service Worker support.
- Protected storage for secrets (Secure Enclave / Trusted Execution Environment).
- Biometrics — desirable but not mandatory.
- At least 80 MB of free space.
- A network connection of 1 Mbit/s or higher.

#### Software conditions
- Confirmed Gosuslugi (ESIA) account.
- Consent to the processing of personal data (FZ-152).

#### Security conditions
- The device must not be compromised (jailbreak/root → refusal to operate).
- Screen protection must be enabled (PIN, biometrics, pattern lock).

### 2.2. User categories
- **Natural persons** — owners of real estate, vehicles, IP, securities.
- **Authorised representatives of legal persons** — for the management of the organisation's assets.
- **Representatives of state enterprises** — for the purposes of corporate programmes.
- **Sole traders, NCOs, foundations** — owners of assets.

## 3. Task description

The task of the programme is to provide the full life cycle of interaction between the asset owner and the Digital Platform CPFSR:

1. Identification — registration through ESIA with confirmation of personal data.
2. Segmentation — determination of the type of ownership.
3. Asset entry — submission to the system with automatic loading of data from state registers.
4. Valuation — request to an authorised partner valuer.
5. Structuring — configuration of smart-contract conditions in line with the target programme.
6. Signing — electronic signature with a qualified enhanced electronic signature.
7. DFA issue — transfer to the Bank of Russia.
8. Emission — targeted funding of funds by the Bank of Russia to escrow.
9. Financing — transfer to the Federal Treasury, implementation of the national project.
10. Monitoring — tracking of smart-contract events.
11. Receipt of income — regular reward payments.
12. Exchange — replacement of the initial DFA with a DFA of the constructed object.
13. Return — removal of the encumbrance upon expiry of the term.
14. Reinvestment (optional) — a new cycle with the same asset.

## 4. Input and output data

### 4.1. Input data
- User's personal data from ESIA.
- Biometric confirmations from EBS.
- User actions (taps, input).
- Data from EGRN/Rospatent/depositary systems.
- Push notifications from the server-side.

### 4.2. Output data
- API requests to the server-side of CPFSR.
- Electronically signed documents (contracts, acts).
- Reports for the user (history, income, tax statements).

## 5. Effectiveness of use

### 5.1. Social and economic effectiveness
- Increased accessibility of the mechanism for attracting private capital to state projects.
- Reduction of the labour intensity of submitting and servicing collateral compared with traditional bank lending by an order of magnitude.
- Reduction of the time from the submission of an asset to the issue of a DFA from 30 days (standard bank collateral) to 1–3 days.
- Preservation of property in the ownership and use of the owner with simultaneous receipt of income.

### 5.2. Technical and economic indicators
- DFA issue time: ≤ 30 seconds from the signing of the smart contract.
- 24/7 availability.
- Supported concurrent user volume: up to 50,000 in parallel.

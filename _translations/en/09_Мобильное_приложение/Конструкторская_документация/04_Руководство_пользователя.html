User manual

CPFSR — mobile application

Identifier: РСФСР.МП.04.РП Standard: GOST 19.505-79

1. Programme purpose

The application is intended for the management of the personal portfolio of assets and digital financial assets (DFAs) on the Digital Platform CPFSR: submission of property as collateral, DFA issue, receipt of income from placement, control of smart contracts.

2. Conditions for programme execution

2.1. Minimum technical requirements

  • iOS 15 and above, or
  • Android 9 (API 28) and above, or
  • Windows 10 (1809) and above, or
  • a modern browser with Service Worker support.

2.2. Preparation for work

  1. Install the application from RuStore, App Store, Google Play, Microsoft Store, or add the PWA to the home screen from the browser.
  2. Prepare a confirmed Gosuslugi account.
  3. Prepare the asset documents (title deeds, cadastral number for real estate).

3. Working with the programme

3.1. First launch

On first launch the application greets the user with a splash screen and automatically goes to the login screen.

Sign-in

Two options are available: - Sign in via Gosuslugi — the main method for new users. OpenID Connect to ESIA with a confirmed account is used. - Biometrics — for repeat sign-ins of an already registered user via Face ID / Touch ID / Windows Hello.

Registration (KYC)

After the initial authorisation, a three-step identity verification is performed: 1. Identity confirmation: Surname, name, patronymic, passport series and number, INN. The data are loaded from a confirmed Gosuslugi account — verify their correctness. 2. Contact details: phone (for SMS codes), email, region. 3. Biometrics: confirmation via the Unified Biometric System. Hold the phone up to your face or place a finger on the fingerprint sensor. You may skip this step and set up biometrics later from the “Settings” menu.

Familiarisation with the capabilities (Onboarding)

After registration, three training screens are shown: 1. Put an asset up as collateral — real estate, vehicles, IP, securities; the property remains with you. 2. The platform issues a DFA — against your asset; the Bank of Russia performs the targeted emission. 3. Receive income — regular payments; on expiry of the term the encumbrance is removed.

Any step may be skipped by tapping “Skip” in the upper-right corner.

3.2. Home screen

The home screen contains: - Balance card — the total value of assets in operation; the number of pledged objects; income for the quarter. - Quick actions: Submit asset, My DFAs, Income, Documents. - National project banner — new financing programmes. - My assets — the two most recent assets with the option to open the full list. - Recent operations — the two most recent operations with the option to open the full history. - Bottom navigation — five tabs: Home, Assets, DFAs, Income, Profile.

3.3. Submission of an asset as collateral

Step 1. Selection of the property type

Available categories: - Real estate (flat, house, apartments); - Land plot; - Vehicle; - Intellectual property; - Securities.

Step 2. Characteristics

For real estate — enter the cadastral number. The address, area, year of construction and other characteristics will be loaded automatically from EGRN through a secured integration channel with Rosreestr (FZ-218). A title deed document is also attached.

Step 3. Valuation request

View the request parameters and confirm the submission. A partner valuation company accredited under FZ-135 will carry out the valuation within 24 hours. The cost of the request is RUB 0 (included in the platform service).

Step 4. Valuation result

Receive the appraisal value and the maximum size of the collateral DFA (50% of the appraisal by default). Collateral insurance is included. Valid for 6 months. Proceed to the smart contract to issue the DFA.

3.4. Configuration and signing of the smart contract

On the smart contract screen, set: - DFA term: 12, 36 or 60 months. - Target use: a national or regional project programme. - DFA par value: automatically calculated as 50% of the appraisal value.

Review the calculated parameters: reward rate, income for the entire term, escrow agent (Bank of Russia), insurance, encumbrance registrar (Rosreestr).

Press “Sign (UKEP)”. An SMS code will be sent to your phone line. Enter the 6-digit code or confirm with biometrics.

After a successful signing, you will see the “DFA successfully issued” screen with a unique DFA identifier. The DFA has been transferred to the Bank of Russia and the encumbrance has been registered in EGRN.

3.5. DFA portfolio management

In the “My DFAs” section, the full list of your DFAs is available with filtering: All, Active, Being issued, Completed. For each DFA, the following is shown: - ID (unique identifier); - par value; - linked asset; - target programme; - term and reward rate; - status.

Clicking a DFA opens a card with detailed information: smart contract conditions, escrow, payments, history. Available actions: download the contract, request a DFA exchange.

3.6. Income

In the “Income” section: - Income balance card: for the year, for the quarter, annual forecast. - Chart of monthly receipts. - List of payments linked to DFAs.

3.7. Operation history

Full operations log with filtering: All, Payments, DFA issue, Exchange. Can be exported to PDF or Excel.

3.8. Notifications

The notifications centre contains: - System messages on payments; - Notifications of new programmes; - Notifications of valuation results; - Notifications of encumbrance registration and other smart-contract events.

Unread notifications are marked with an accent dot. Tapping the bell allows navigation to all notifications. Tapping the “⊘” icon marks all notifications as read.

3.9. Profile

The “Profile” section provides: - Personal data. - Payout details. - My documents. - Notifications. - Platform documents. - Help and support. - Sign-out from the account.

3.10. Settings

Available: - Language: Russian (default), English, French, Arabic. - Theme: light, dark, soft, high-contrast for the visually impaired. - Font size: smaller, normal, larger. - Security: biometrics, PIN code, two-factor authentication. - Notifications: push notifications.

3.11. Platform documents

The section contains links to the full documentation of the CPFSR project: documents hub, big presentation, small presentation, website, legal foundation, international valuation, DD audit, expert opinion.

3.12. Support

Available: FAQ, chat with the operator, support phone, feedback form.

4. Operator messages

4.1. Message types

  • Push notifications — displayed on the device’s home screen.
  • In-app notifications — displayed in the notifications centre.
  • Messages from Gosuslugi — displayed in the standard Gosuslugi service.

4.2. Actions on messages

Each notification contains a call to action (e.g. “Confirm payout”, “Start DFA issue”). Tap the notification to open the corresponding screen.

5. Error messages

Code Message User action
AUTH-01 Identity could not be confirmed via Gosuslugi Try again, check access to Gosuslugi
AUTH-02 Biometrics not confirmed Enter the confirmation code or confirm via EBS
ASSET-01 Object not found in EGRN Check the correctness of the cadastral number
ASSET-02 The object is already pledged to another organisation Remove the previous encumbrance
CFA-01 The DFA could not be issued Contact support
NET-01 No connection to the server Check the Internet; data will be restored after the connection is restored
SIGN-01 Electronic signature rejected Check the validity of the certificate

The document complies with the requirements of GOST 19.505-79 “Operator manual”.

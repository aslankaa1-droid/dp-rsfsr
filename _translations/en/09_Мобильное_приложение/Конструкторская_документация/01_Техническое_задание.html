Terms of Reference for the development of the mobile application

CPFSR — Russian Service for Social Development Financing

Identifier: РСФСР.МП.01.ТЗ Standard: GOST 19.201-78 Date: 2025 Rightsholder: Abdul-Khakim Akhmadovich Kagirov (Deposit certificate No. 4011265 of 19.12.2024)

1. Introduction

1.1. Name of the programme

Full: “Digital Platform CPFSR — mortgagor’s mobile application”. Identifier: РСФСР.МП.

1.2. Scope of application

The mobile application is the client software of the Digital Platform CPFSR — the operator of the issue of digital financial assets (DFAs) against the collateral of property of natural and legal persons. In the pilot-light regime of 2026–2028, the targeted funding of national and infrastructure projects against the DFA pool is organised through the VEB.RF Project Finance Factory and the Federal Treasury; the Bank of Russia acts as the general supervisory regulator of the financial market under Art. 4 of FZ-86. On the horizon of 2029+ — expansion of the mandate upon the adoption of the regulatory acts package.

1.3. The programme is intended for

  • registration and identification of mortgagors through ESIA and EBS;
  • submission of property as collateral (real estate, vehicles, intellectual property, securities);
  • signing of smart contracts for the issue of DFAs using a qualified enhanced electronic signature;
  • management of the DFA portfolio and receipt of income from placement;
  • receipt of notifications on key smart-contract events (DFA exchange, payments, insurance events).

2. Basis for development

  • Author’s concept, deposited under No. 4011265 of 19.12.2024.
  • Federal Law No. 259-FZ of 31.07.2020 “On Digital Financial Assets”.
  • Bank of Russia Regulation No. 779-P of 16.11.2021.
  • Decree of the President of the Russian Federation No. 166 of 30.03.2022 (import substitution).

3. Purpose of the development

3.1. Functional purpose

To provide the asset owner with mobile access to all functions of the CPFSR platform with an availability level of 99.99% and a business operation response time of no more than 200 ms (P95).

3.2. Operational purpose

Operated on devices running: - iOS 15 and above; - Android 9 (API 28) and above; - Windows 10 (1809) and above; - web browsers with Service Workers and WebAssembly support (PWA mode).

4. Requirements for the programme

4.1. Functional requirements

4.1.1. Identification and authentication

  • Authorisation through the Unified Identification and Authentication System (ESIA) via the OpenID Connect protocol.
  • Authorisation through the Unified Biometric System (Bank of Russia Regulation No. 482-P) — face and/or voice.
  • Local authentication by device biometrics (Face ID, Touch ID, Windows Hello) or PIN code.
  • Two-factor authentication for critical operations (SMS code + biometrics).

4.1.2. Mortgagor registration (KYC)

  • Loading personal data from a confirmed Gosuslugi account.
  • Verification against the AML/CTF registers (Federal Law No. 115-FZ).
  • Phone and email confirmation.
  • Biometrics binding for the signing of operations.

4.1.3. Asset management

  • Submission of an asset as collateral (4-step wizard: type → characteristics → documents → submission for valuation).
  • Automatic loading of characteristics from EGRN (Rosreestr), the Rospatent register, depositary systems.
  • Viewing the list of own assets with filtering.
  • Asset card with operation history, encumbrance status, insurance.

4.1.4. DFA issue and servicing

  • Smart contract designer (term, target purpose, reward rate).
  • Signing with a qualified enhanced electronic signature (GOST R 34.10-2012).
  • Operation confirmation via an SMS code or EBS biometrics.
  • Viewing active DFAs, incoming payments, history.

4.1.5. Income and history

  • Chart of monthly receipts over the period.
  • History of all operations (issue, payments, exchanges, insurance events).
  • Tax statements (2-NDFL) — auto-generation and download.
  • Export of the history to PDF and Excel.

4.1.6. Notifications

  • Push notifications on key events.
  • In-app notification centre.
  • Integration with Gosuslugi for the receipt of official notifications.

4.1.7. Profile and settings

  • Management of contact details.
  • Management of security (biometrics, PIN, two-factor authentication).
  • Management of notification subscriptions.
  • Payout details.

4.1.8. Documents and support

  • Access to the Information System Rules (Article 6 of FZ-259).
  • Access to the platform’s project documentation.
  • Chat with the support operator.
  • FAQ.

4.2. Reliability requirements

Parameter Value
Application availability (online) 99.9%
Offline mode (cache view) yes
Recovery time after a failure ≤ 5 seconds
Data integrity GOST R 34.11-2012 checksums

4.3. Information security requirements

  • Local storage of sensitive data only in protected storages: iOS Keychain, Android Keystore, Windows Credential Locker.
  • Network interaction exclusively over TLS with the use of GOST certificates (KriptoPro TLS).
  • Protection against compromise: jailbreak/root detection, anti-debug, certificate pinning.
  • No storage of biometric vectors on the device — only identifiers for verification through EBS.
  • Compliance with the OWASP MASVS standard at level L2 and above.
  • Compliance with the requirements of Bank of Russia Regulation No. 683-P, Instruction No. 6635-U.
  • CII category — 1st (highest).

4.4. Performance requirements

Parameter Value
Cold start time ≤ 2 seconds on 4G
Interface response time ≤ 100 ms
Distribution size ≤ 80 MB
RAM consumption ≤ 300 MB
Background energy consumption minimal

4.5. Interface requirements

  • Compliance with the requirements of WCAG 2.2 level AA (accessibility).
  • Theme support: light, dark, soft for long viewing, high-contrast for the visually impaired.
  • Support for four languages: Russian (primary), English, French, Arabic (with RTL layout).
  • Support for font scaling.
  • Support for voice assistive technologies (VoiceOver, TalkBack, Narrator).
  • Minimum touch area size — 44 × 44 pixels.

4.6. Requirements for the composition and parameters of technical means

Client devices

  • iOS: iPhone 8 and newer; iPad 5th generation and newer.
  • Android: devices with a screen resolution of 360 × 640 pixels or higher, RAM of 3 GB and above.
  • Windows: desktop and tablet devices with a resolution of 1280 × 720 pixels and above.
  • PWA: any devices with Service Worker support (Chrome 70+, Safari 13+, Edge 80+, Firefox 70+).

Network requirements

  • Minimum: 4G LTE or Wi-Fi 80 Mbit/s.
  • Recommended: 5G or Wi-Fi 6.

4.7. Information and software compatibility requirements

  • Register of Russian software (RF Government Resolution No. 1236) — the application is subject to inclusion.
  • Inclusion in the RuStore application catalogue.
  • Possibility of installation via App Store / Google Play / Microsoft Store subject to regulatory approvals.
  • Integration with ESIA (RF Government Resolution No. 584).
  • Integration with the Unified Biometric System (Bank of Russia Regulation No. 482-P).

4.8. Marking and packaging requirements

Not applicable (software).

4.9. Transportation and storage requirements

Not applicable.

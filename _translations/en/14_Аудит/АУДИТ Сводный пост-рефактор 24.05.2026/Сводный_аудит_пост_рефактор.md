## 1. Executive Summary

The refactor that followed the clarifying audit of 24.05.2026 (morning edition) was completed at **52% effective closure of what the log claims**. Strategically valuable shifts are visible: the concept has become two-stage (Stage 1 wedge / pilot-light 2026–2028 without 86-FZ amendments + Stage 2 horizon 2029+), § 8 of the Legal Foundation has been split into 8.1/8.2, a contractual package of 5 framework drafts has been put together, biometrics has been rewritten under 572-FZ + CBT JSC, GDPR has been removed, the GOST-19 design documentation set has been completed to 13 documents with correct numbering, the Acceptance Test Programme has been enlarged with 5 crypto scenarios FT-021…FT-025 at MASVS L2+, and a single SoT v3 is fixed in `_Мастер/Единые_показатели_v2.md` and synchronised in `_shared/i18n.js` across four locales and in the Pitch Deck. The architectural fault line — wedge vs emission model — is closed at the first tier of communication artefacts.

At the same time, the refactor log declares closed a number of items for which actual closure is absent or façade-only. The heaviest: **(a)** 15+ client HTML/MD files (Финмодель .md/.html, Business Plan .md/.html, DD Report .md/.html, Large Deck, Small Deck, Международная_оценка .md/.html, Expert Opinion, the root index.html partially) **retain the pre-refactor financial KPIs** — NPV ₽170 / ₽96.8 bn, IRR ~228%, WACC 22%, Equity Multiple 48× / 781×, EBITDA TV 96.9%, Payback 2.4 years, post-money $75M, Reasoned $35–95M, RF 13.5%, ERP 6%, KS 18% (in the sensitivity baseline); **(b)** the EULA at `09_Мобильное_приложение/Публикация_RuStore/03_Условия_использования.md:101` retains a cap that is void under art. 16 of the Consumer Rights Protection Act ("commissions over 12 months"), which blocks RuStore publication; **(c)** SoT v3 contains its own TV arithmetic error — Phases I+II of the Multi-stage TV are declared as ₽7.4 bn, while the line-by-line recomputation yields ₽21.0 bn (×2.84), which moves the defensible full NPV from 51.2 to ₽64.7 bn; **(d)** Business Plan §§ 1, 5, 21, 23, DD Report §§ 2.1, 3.3, 10 and CBR Address §§ 3.1, 6, 10 continue to describe the Stage 2 emission model as the operational baseline, without an explicit horizon marker; **(e)** the live PWA at `09_Мобильное_приложение/Приложение/index.html:1033,1513` still contains the phrase "the Bank of Russia carries out a targeted emission" in the onboarding wizard and the status card; **(f)** the mobile application specification at `09_Мобильное_приложение/Спецификация_мобильного_приложения.md:12` literally declares the stack as "React Native 0.74+ with TypeScript", whereas the actual code is built through Capacitor.

Communication readiness: the package is fit for friendly capital and an amicable strategic investor on condition of an oral caveat about the refactor status and the delivery of a reduced set (SoT v3 + Pitch Deck + Concept + Wedge_pilot-light + Large Deck). For Gazprombank-DD / VEB.RF IPF / tier-1 VC the package is not ready: the first week of DD will expose the fault line "Business Plan/DD Report speak Stage 2 — Concept/decks speak Stage 1" and mutually exclusive financial KPIs across artefacts.

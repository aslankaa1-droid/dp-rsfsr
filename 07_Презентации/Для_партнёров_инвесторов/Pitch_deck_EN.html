Problem

₽25 trillion of real estate collateral is frozen out of capital markets

The structural gap

  • Real estate value: ₽507 trillion across residential, commercial, land in RF.
  • Conservative collateral capacity: ₽25 trillion (5% mobilization × 50% LTV).
  • Currently mobilized: < 0.5% via traditional mortgage securitization.
  • Result: ₽12 national projects (2026–2030 budget ₽13.2T) are financed almost exclusively from federal budget — adding pressure on OFZ.

Why current solutions fail

  • Traditional securitization — too slow, too expensive for retail-grade assets.
  • Bank loans — concentrate risk on individual balance sheets.
  • Existing CFA platforms (Atomyze, Sber CFA, Alfa CFA) — focus on corporate, not real estate collateral.
  • No regulated path for tokenizing real estate against national project financing.
